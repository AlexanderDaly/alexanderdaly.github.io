Behavior Rules

Bug. Slow wander sets intention; fast jitter bends each wingbeat; nectar pulls gently; sudden disturbance creates a sharp escape arc.

Leaf. The breeze is a layered noise field; gusts add torque; inertia makes the leaf lag behind the air instead of obeying it instantly.
